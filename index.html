This is the new current based neuron simulation. It attempts to mimic the neurons in the brain using the standard integrate and fire model (with constants modeled after the real world).

click to create a new neuron
shift click to create an inhibitory neuron
delete to delete a current neuron and all its connections
space to switch the graphed neuron
esc to unselect
right click to activate a neuron
